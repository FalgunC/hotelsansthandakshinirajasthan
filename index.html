About Us

Established in 2019, Hotel Sansthan Dakshini Rajasthan is a hotel owners' association representing the hospitality industry across the seven districts of Southern Rajasthan—Udaipur, Dungarpur, Banswara, Rajsamand, Pratapgarh, Chittorgarh, and Salumber—collectively known as the Mewar region.

The association is committed to protecting the rights of hotel owners, promoting responsible tourism growth, and ensuring the sustainable development of the hospitality sector in this culturally rich region.

Led by hotel owners, our Executive Committee brings together local experience, unity, and a shared vision. We also serve as a bridge between government authorities and the hotel industry, keeping members updated on policies, schemes, compliance rules, and tourism-related opportunities.

Our mission is to build a strong, unified voice for hoteliers and to elevate the Mewar region as a premier tourism destination in India.
